🚀 Why You Should Switch to Tredecim

The Tredecim Calendar isn’t just a novelty — it’s a practical, thoughtful reform of the way we measure time. Here’s why you should consider adopting it:

🔧 Fixing Flaws in the Gregorian Calendar

  • ✅ 28-day months = no partial weeks, no floating weekdays.
  • ✅ Intermission day = clean leap year handling with no weekday shift.
  • ✅ Restored logic: September is month 7, October is 8, etc.
  • ✅ Always know what weekday any date falls on.

📐 Mathematically Clean

  • 13 × 28 = 364 days + 1 extra day in February + 1 Intermission (every 4 years).
  • Each week fits perfectly in each month — no drift. February has one extra day to balance the solar year.
  • Cleaner timekeeping for schools, businesses, and developers.

🎯 Predictable & Easy to Use

  • Every month has exactly 4 weeks — never worry about month length.
  • Weekday logic becomes intuitive: the 1st of every month is the same weekday.
  • No need to memorize irregular leap year rules or months with 30/31 days.

🌐 Transition Support

  • Converter tools let you map Gregorian dates instantly.
  • Holiday mapping helps preserve global traditions on the Tredecim system.
  • Printable and digital versions available.
  • Subscribe to it as a calendar on your device via ICS feed.

🎉 Bonus: Seasonal Alignment & Celebration

  • Dedicated Intermission holiday every 4 years.
  • Built-in week-long summer celebration (June 18–24).
  • Month names feel meaningful again.
