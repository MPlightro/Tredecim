Welcome to the Tredecim Calendar

A logical, beautiful, and mathematically consistent calendar system.

Quick Overview

  • 🗓️ 13 months per year, each with 28 days (except February: 29)
  • 🌍 Year starts on August 1st
  • 📆 Intermission Day every 4 years (not part of any month)
  • 🔁 Weekdays align across every month
  • 🔢 Month names reordered for logical and linguistic consistency
