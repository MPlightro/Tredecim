❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Why start the year on August 1st?

It gives time to prepare and aligns the 13-month cycle cleanly with a modern adoption window, especially starting from a leap year.

Why does February have 29 days?

February is kept special, holding the 365th day in non-leap years — maintaining a sense of tradition and balance.

How does Intermission work?

It appears every 4 years after July 28, acting as a weekday buffer before August 1. It's not part of any month but keeps weekdays aligned year to year.

Will existing holidays be supported?

Yes! The Tredecim Calendar maps major holidays to fixed or weekday-based equivalents. See the "Holidays" section for details.

Is there a way to sync with my phone?

Yes, you can subscribe to the calendar as a web-based .ics feed to view Tredecim dates in your calendar app.

How do I convert dates?

The converter tool lets you switch between Gregorian and Tredecim formats with weekday alignment preserved.
